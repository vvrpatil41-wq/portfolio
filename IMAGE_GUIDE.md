## Image Requirements

- **Format:** JPG or PNG recommended
- **Resolution:** Minimum 1200px width for best quality
- **Aspect Ratio:** Images will be cropped to fit gallery containers
- **File Size:** Optimize for web (under 500KB per image recommended)
